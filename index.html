Comparing Game Types: MOBAs, FPS, and Story Based Games

Published Date: June 9, 2024

Author: Your Name

Video games have many subgenres in this post we will go through 3 of them. MOBAs which stands for multiplayer online battle arena, FPS or first person shooter, and Story based games which are typically singleplayer.

MOBAs are a strategy team based game where players work together toward a goal against another team. FPS games come in many different varients but all focus on a players ability to aim and shoot other players and story based games focus mostly on a plot, character development, and an immersive expirience.

Genre Main Features Popular Examples
MOBA Team-based strategy, character roles, objectives League of Legends, Dota 2
FPS First-person perspective, weapon combat, fast-paced Call of Duty, Overwatch
Story-Based Narrative focus, character development, immersive worlds The Witcher 3, The Last of Us

Each genre offers unique experiences. MOBAs require strategic thinking and teamwork. FPS games demand quick reflexes and accuracy. Story based games provide a narrative and a plot that immerses the player.
